Lure of the Crowdfund

Crowdfunding is not a new idea but the recent passing of the JOBS act in the US (Jumpstart Our Business Startups), a series of 6 bills designed to make it easier for startups to gain access to capital, has been the catalyst for excitement and speculation among entrepreneurs trying to fund their next big ideas. With many traditional VC firms being understandably more cautious in recent years and favouring to invest in later-stage companies, the potential for crowdfund investing to make a major impact on the number of early-stage startups being able to finance their ideas is massive.
