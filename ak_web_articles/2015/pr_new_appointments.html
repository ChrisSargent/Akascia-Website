Akascia adds San Francisco Presence

Sebastian Steinbach joins our advisory board.

▼

San Francisco / Sophia Antipolis / Sydney

Akascia are delighted to announce the appointment of Sebastian Steinbach to our Advisory Board in San Francisco. Akascia is an international executive search firm focused on serving fast-growing internet and telecom startups. Already active in most of Europe and Asia, Akascia has also been supporting Silicon Valley companies and is increasing its local presence in the SF Bay Area. Growing quickly into new markets has never been as important as it is today says Sebastian Steinbach, former CEO of international executive search firm Steinbach & Partner. Entrepreneurs have a drive to build. Accessing new markets defines success for them. The executive recruiter's job is to build the right teams, to make sure those plans work out. Sebastian is also active in the startup community in San Francisco and currently serves as the Development Director for the Cleantech Open, the world's largest cleantech accelerator.

Sebastian's appointment underlines our commitment to building a world-class executive search team at Akascia to service the most ambitious startups and fast-growing companies in the world. He knows what it takes to build executive teams and he has worked with clients on five continents. states Chris Sargent, Akascia's Head of European and US operations.
