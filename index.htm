Our Company

We are an international, executive search firm focused on helping technology companies grow their business in EMEA and APAC.

We don't pretend to have a revolutionary way of helping you win the "War for Talent" and out of respect for our customers and ourselves, we don't use unqualified superlatives to describe what we do. We don't over complicate what we do and we don't get distracted by new fads and trends in talent management.

The essence and strength of our firm is in our passion and approach to executive search and the way in which we deal with our clients and candidates. That is why you should work with us.

If you insist on comparing us to other search firms, there are some things we'd like to say about ourselves:

  • Executive search, purely done. To us, this means three things: We only do executive search, we only do it using proven headhunting and networking techniques and we just get it done.
  • We don't boast about a "75% completion rate"; we'd be embarrassed if it was that low.
  • We enjoy being disruptive and challenging. We believe the only way to stand out is to stand for something special. Once you start working with us, you'll know what we mean.
  • All our searches are Director-led, we have a select client base to reduce off-limits issues and we only take on searches we know we can complete within your timescales.
  • We haven't worked in blue-chip, managerial roles and we have an aversion to wearing suits! However, we are experts in search, finding the un-findable and we know what types of people it takes to run a successful business.
  • We are supported by an advisory board with more than a century's worth of expertise from key disciplines in the technology arena. Read more.

Our Approach

Our approach to working with you is simply described; we treat people decently, with courtesy, honesty, openness and humility.

Our approach to executive search is thorough and considered. We believe it is the most effective way of finding the very best people in the industry. To maintain your belief in this, and therefore us, we approach every search assignment with a large commitment of our time and resources to ensure a return on your investment.

Accordingly, we also have certain expectations of you and your commitment to hiring the best people. For example, some of the important questions we'll expect to be answered before we start include:

  • Are you serious about improving the quality of people your company employs?
  • Do you care that our short and long-term goals are shared?
  • Do you understand that the success of our search somewhat depends on prompt and high quality feedback from you?
  • Can you describe what your new employee will have achieved and how they will have progressed by the end of their first, second and fifth years?
  • Do you have realistic expectations of who you can attract and are you sure they exist?
  • Do you trust your own intuition when it comes to an individual's potential impact on your organisation's success?
  • Do you appreciate that we won't treat you or our candidates as commodities and we value our own and our candidates' time as much as we value yours?
  • Do you realise that, in recruitment, timing and momentum are critical?
  • Do you recognise that with nearly 30 years' experience in search, we're starting to get the hang of it?
  • Are you being completely honest with us...?

Our Passion

Getting it right is our passion.

Our goal of satisfying the needs of the most discerning, demanding executives in their search for new people drives us beyond any single transaction. We're also passionate about our approach; that of being open and casual whilst remaining absolutely serious about what we do.

We relish being an agonising thorn in the side of our traditional, obstinate competitors like Korn Ferry, Heidrick & Struggles and Spencer Stuart.

Our passion and approach are the essence of our firm and the following points help to define that a little more.

  • We aim to contradict the stereotypical, fast-talking recruiters and we are far from being corporate animals! (If you want a corporate machine churning through your searches, you should probably use one of our competitors.)
  • We like to think that we are the best executive search firm in our field yet we still wake up every day thinking of ways to be better.
  • Being only better than our competitors is simply not good enough.
  • Like your best employees, we don't give up until we deliver results.
  • We are entrepreneurs and we like to work with people like us; people who are serious about what they do, have acute attention to detail and do everything to the best of their ability.
  • We love finding the best people for the best jobs and the thought of making your company more successful whilst doing it.
  • We appreciate that knowing what our customers need, goes beyond understanding what they want... then we give them both.
  • We trust that our passion for what we do will inspire you to hire better people.
